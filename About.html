About InternMatch

Your Smart Gateway to Internship Opportunities

Who We Are

InterMatch is a dedicated platform connecting students and young professionals with internship opportunities under various Prime Minister schemes. Our goal is to make skill development and practical experience accessible, relevant, and tailored to each individual. Through InterMatch, students can create profiles highlighting their skills, interests, and resumes, allowing them to find internships that truly match their career aspirations. We simplify the process of discovering government-backed programs, ensuring that every opportunity leads to meaningful learning and growth. At InterMatch, we don’t just list internships—we create pathways for students to gain experience, build confidence, and shape their future careers..

Our Mission

We aim to create equal access for all students across India by matching opportunities with their unique profiles (skills, education, and interests).
