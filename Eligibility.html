General Eligibility Criteria

  • Academic Status: Must be a student pursuing UG/PG degree from a recognized institution.
  • Nationality: Must be a national of the country offering the internship.
  • Age: Specific age range (e.g., 18–25 years).
  • Educational Background – Relevant course/stream (e.g., B.Tech for IT internships, BBA/MBA for management internships).
  • Academic Performance: Minimum 60% percentage/CGPA in previous exams.
  • Employment Status: Should not be employed full-time.
  • No Prior Government Apprenticeships: Not completed any funded skill development program.

Commonly Required Documents

  • Academic Documents: Resume, marksheets, transcripts, degree certificates.
  • Identification: Aadhaar card, Proof of Identity, Proof of Address, Proof of DOB.
  • Institutional Documents: Bonafide certificate / College ID.
